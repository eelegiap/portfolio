About me:

I'm a rising senior at Harvard studying Computer Science and Slavic languages and literature. When I'm not agonizing over Jupyter Notebooks or parsing large textual datasets, you can find me reading Tolstoy and Dostoevsky or trying to pet the neighborhood cats. I pursue research at the intersection of language, computation, and media studies, by using tools from natural language processing, data analytics, and media & literary theory.


Current positions:

  • Current researcher on disinformation on social media platforms with the Social Media Lab at Stanford's Center for the Study of Language and Information.
  • Former researcher with Prof. Amelia Glaser at the Harvard Radcliffe Institute for Advanced Study on her project creating an archive of Ukrainian poetry posted to Facebook after 2014.
  • Former researcher with Prof. Steven Clancy on his Visualizing Russian project. Feel free to check it out!
  • Former president of the Harvard Technology Review
