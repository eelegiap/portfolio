About me:

I'm currently in my third year at Harvard College studying Computer Science and Slavic languages and literature. When I'm not agonizing over my Python code or parsing extremely large textual datasets, you can find me reading Tolstoy and Dostoevsky or trying to pet cats I meet on the street. I pursue research at the intersection of language, computation, and media studies, by using tools from natural language processing, data analytics, and media & literary theory.
